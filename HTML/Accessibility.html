Accessibility

As the creators of this website, we are committed to making it accessible to visitors with disabilities and are continually working to increase its accessibility and usability. To help us make our website a positive place for everyone, we've been using the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 from the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities, and user-friendly for everyone. The guidelines have three levels of accessibility (A, AA and AAA) and we’ve chosen Level AA as the target for our website.

We've worked hard on our website and towards achieving our goal of Level AA compliance. We monitor the website regularly to maintain this goal. If you do find any problems, please contact us by accesing the 'Contact' page in the menu to the left.
